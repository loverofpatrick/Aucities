Townsville

Townsville is a city on the north-eastern coast of Queensland, Australia. With a population of 180,820 as of June 2018, it is the largest settlement in North Queensland; it is unofficially considered its capital.Townsville hosts a significant number of governmental, community and major business administrative offices for the northern half of the state.

Temperature

The best months for a swim in Townsville City, Australia, are October and November, with a perfect temperature - varying between 25°C (77°F) and 29°C (84.2°F), and scarce rainfall.
January through March, November and December, with an average high-temperature of 31°C (87.8°F), are warmest months.

The coldest months in Townsville City are June and July, with an average high-temperature of 25°C (77°F).

With an average of 10h of sunshine, October has the most sunshine of the year. February, March and May, with an average of 7h of sunshine, are months with the least sunshine in Townsville City, Australia.

Rain

In Townsville City, during the entire year, the rain falls for 83 days and collects up to 1115mm (43.9") of precipitation.

January is the month with the most rainfall in Townsville City. Rain falls for 11 days and accumulates 274mm (10.79") of precipitation.

September is the month with the least rainfall. Rain falls for 3 days and accumulates 10mm (0.39") of precipitation.

Average Salary

The average salary in Townsville, Queensland is AU$25.76 kper hour and AU$66,000 per year, according to Payscale statistics by November 2022.

The most popular occupations in Townsville, Queensland are Operations Manager, Accountant, and Retail Store Manager, which pay between AU$46,000 and AU$112,000 per year.

The most popular employers are Glencore Ltd., Aecom Corporation, and Townsville City Council.

House Price

The median House sales price is AU $370,000, and the median house rent is $530 per week, according to Realestate market survey in November 2022.

Ancestry

The most common ancestries in Townsville were Australian 28.8%, English 26.7%, Irish 9.0%, Scottish 7.3% and Italian 4.7%.

Country of birth

79.5% of people were born in Australia. The most common countries of birth were England 2.4%, New Zealand 2.1%, Philippines 0.8%, India 0.6% and Italy 0.5%.

Religion

In Townsville, Christianity was the largest religious group reported overall (68.0%).

The most common responses for religion in Townsville were Catholic 28.3%, No Religion, so described 26.2%, Anglican 15.6%, Not stated 10.1% and Uniting Church 5.7%.
