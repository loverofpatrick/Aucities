Gold Coast

The Gold Coast is a coastal city in the state of Queensland, Australia, approximately 66 kilometres south-southeast of the centre of the state capital Brisbane. With a population over 600,000, the Gold Coast is the sixth-largest city in Australia, the nation's largest regional city, and Queensland's second-largest city after Brisbane

Average Salary

The average salary in Gold Coast, Queensland is AU$25.69 per hour and AU$61,000 per year, according to Payscale statistics by November 2022.

The most popular occupations in Gold Coast, Queensland are Operations Manager, Accountant, and Retail Store Manager, which pay between AU$46,000 and AU$98,000 per year.

The most popular employers are Metricon Homes, Boardriders, and G8 Education Limited.

Ancestry

The most common ancestries in Gold Coast were English 29.3%, Australian 22.5%, Irish 8.2%, Scottish 7.5% and German 3.6%.

Country of birth

In Gold Coast, 64.0% of people were born in Australia. The most common countries of birth were New Zealand 7.9%, England 5.2%, China (excludes SARs and Taiwan) 1.2%, South Africa 1.2% and Japan 0.7%.

Religion

In Gold Coast, Christianity was the largest religious group reported overall (59.5%).

The most common responses for religion in Gold Coast were No Religion, so described 31.8%, Catholic 20.7%, Anglican 16.2%, Not stated 10.4% and Uniting Church 3.9%.
