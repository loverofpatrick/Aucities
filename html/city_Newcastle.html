Newcastle

The City of Newcastle is located in the Hunter Region of New South Wales, about 160 kilometres north of Sydney. In 2021 this region had a total population of 619,653. Newcastle is often quoted as being the seventh largest city in Australia.

Temperature

The warmest month in Newcastle, Australia, is January, with an average high-temperature of 26.7°C (80.1°F) and an average low-temperature of 20.3°C (68.5°F).

With an average high-temperature of 16.3°C (61.3°F) and an average low-temperature of 10.9°C (51.6°F), July is the coldest month in Newcastle.

The months with the most sunshine in Newcastle, Australia, are November and December, with an average of 9.8h of sunshine, and June has the least sunshine of the year, with an average of 5.8h of sunshine.

Rain

Throughout the year, in Newcastle, there are 160.9 rainfall days, and 620mm (24.41") of precipitation is accumulated.

The months with the lowest humidity are August and September, with an average relative humidity of 69%. August is the month with the least rainfall in Newcastle. Rain falls for 10.2 days and accumulates 34mm (1.34") of precipitation.

February is the most humid month, with an average relative humidity of 77% as it is the month with the most rainfall in Newcastle. Rain falls for 14.7 days and accumulates 79mm (3.11") of precipitation.

Average Salary

The average salary in Newcastle, New South Wales is AU$26.51 per hour and AU$67,000 per year, according to Payscale statistics by November 2022.

The most popular occupations in Newcastle, New South Wales are Carpenter, Dental Assistant, and Registered Nurse (RN) which pay between AU$20.36 and AU$39.28 per hour.

The most popular employers in Newcastle , New South Wales are Woolworths, Anytime Fitness, Inc., and NSW Health.

House Price

The median House sales price is AU$1,022,000-$1,500,000 for houses with various bedrooms, and the median house rent is $700, according to Realestate market survey in November 2022.

Unit and Apartment Price

The median Unit and Apartment sales price is AU$385,000, and the median house rent is AU$667 per week for apartments with 2 bedrooms, according to Realestate market survey in November 2022

Ancestry

The most common ancestries in Newcastle were English 28.6%, Australian 19.5%, Irish 11.1%, Scottish 9.9% and German 3.8%.

Country of birth

In Newcastle, 69.1% of people were born in Australia. The most common countries of birth were England 3.4%, New Zealand 1.4%, United States of America 1.3%, Germany 0.8% and China (excludes SARs and Taiwan) 0.8%.

Religion

The most common responses for religion in Newcastle were No Religion 31.1%, Catholic 21.7% and Anglican 19.2%.
