Bendigo

The City of Greater Bendigo is located in north-central Victoria, about 150 kilometres north-west of the Melbourne CBD. Its Estimated Resident Population for 2021 is 121,221.

Temperature

With an average high-temperature of 30.4°C (86.7°F) and an average low-temperature of 15.7°C (60.3°F), January is the warmest month.

With an average high-temperature of 12.1°C (53.8°F) and an average low-temperature of 4.3°C (39.7°F), July is the coldest month in Bendigo.

With an average of 10.3h of sunshine, December has the most sunshine of the year. With an average of 6.5h of sunshine, July has the least sunshine of the year in Bendigo.

Rain

In Bendigo, Australia, during the entire year, the rain falls for 109.6 days and collects up to 323mm (12.72") of precipitation.

The most humid month in Bendigo is July, with an average relative humidity of 85%. August is the month with the most rainfall. Rain falls for 13.4 days and accumulates 35mm (1.38") of precipitation.

The least humid month in Bendigo, Australia, is January, with an average relative humidity of 48%. March is the month with the least rainfall. Rain falls for 7.3 days and accumulates 22mm (0.87") of precipitation.

Average Salary

The average salary in Bendigo, Victoria is AU$25.57 per hour and AU$64,000 per year, according to Payscale statistics by November 2022.

The most popular occupations in Bendigo, Victoria are Carpenter, Electrician, and Personal Care Assistant (PCA) which pay between AU$20.01 and AU$35.88 per hour.

The most popular employers are Bendigo Health, Hazelden, and Champions IGA.

House Price

The median House sales price is AU $577,500-$977,500 for houses with 2-4 bedrooms, and the median house rent is $387-$450 per week for houses with 2-4 bedrooms, according to Realestate market survey in November 2022.

Unit and Apartment Price

The median Unit and Apartment sales price is $400,000, and the median rent is $220-AU$470 per week for apartments with 1-3 bedrooms, according to Realestate market survey in November 2022

Ancestry

The most common ancestries in Bendigo were English 30.6%, Australian 30.1%, Irish 10.3%, Scottish 8.4% and German 3.3%.

Country of birth

82.8% of people were born in Australia. The most common countries of birth were England 2.3%, New Zealand 0.8%, India 0.5%, Philippines 0.4% and Scotland 0.3%.

Religion

In Bendigo, Christianity was the largest religious group reported overall (55.4%).

The most common responses for religion in Bendigo were No Religion, so described 37.1%, Catholic 20.6%, Anglican 12.9%, Not stated 10.7% and Uniting Church 6.5%.
