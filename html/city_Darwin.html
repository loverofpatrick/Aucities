Darwin

Darwin is the capital city of the Northern Territory, Australia. With an estimated population of 147,255 as of 2019, the city contains the majority of the residents of the sparsely populated Northern Territory.

Temperature

November is the warmest month, with an average high-temperature of 33.3°C (91.9°F) and an average low-temperature of 27.6°C (81.7°F).

With an average high-temperature of 29.4°C (84.9°F) and an average low-temperature of 21.7°C (71.1°F), July is the coldest month.

The month with the least sunshine in Darwin, Australia, is January, with an average of 7.5h of sunshine. June and August, with an average of 9.9h of sunshine, are months with the most sunshine in Darwin, Australia.

Rain

In Darwin, Australia, during the entire year, the rain falls for 191.8 days and collects up to 1282mm (50.47") of precipitation.

January is the month with the most rainfall in Darwin. Rain falls for 28.3 days and accumulates 340mm (13.39") of precipitation. The months with the highest humidity are January and February, with an average relative humidity of 78%. June is the least humid month, with an average relative humidity of 57%. The months with the least rainfall in Darwin, Australia, are June through August Rain falls for 1.9 days and accumulates 2mm (0.08") of rain.

Average Salary

The average salary in Darwin, Northern Territory is AU$27.04 per hour and AU$69,000 per year, according to Payscale statistics by November 2022.

The most popular occupations in Darwin, Northern Territory are Early Childhood Educator (ECE), Registered Nurse (RN), and Educator, which pay between AU$20.55 and AU$40.81 per hour.

The most popular employers are Woolworths, Harvey Norman, and Royal Darwin Hospital.

House Price

The median House sales price is $588,053, and the median house rent is $612.25 per week for 3-bedroom houses, according to Realestate market survey in November 2022.

Unit and Apartment Price

The median Unit and Apartment sales price is $376,428 or $240,000-$535,000 for apartments with 1 to 3 bedrooms, and the median rent is $430-$700 per week for apartments with 1 to 3 bedrooms, according to Realestate market survey in November 2022

Ancestry

The most common ancestries in Darwin were Australian 24.5%, English 21.5%, Irish 7.3%, Scottish 5.8% and German 3.3%.

Country of birth

In Darwin, 62.7% of people were born in Australia. The most common countries of birth were Philippines 3.6%, England 3.1%, New Zealand 2.1%, India 2.0% and Greece 0.9%.

Religion

In Darwin, Christianity was the largest religious group reported overall (52.6%). The most common responses for religion in Darwin were No Religion, so described 32.8%, Catholic 21.6%, Not stated 14.7%, Anglican 8.7% and Uniting Church 3.2%.
